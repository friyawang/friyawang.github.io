About Me

I am an incoming lecturer at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (NJUPT). Currently, I am pursuing my Ph.D. at Fudan University, expected to graduate in Fall 2025. I received my bachelor's degree from Xidian University.

Research Interests

My research focuses on energy harvesting systems, particularly:

  • Piezoelectric Energy Harvesting Interface Circuits
  • Power Management Integrated Circuits
  • Energy-Efficient Circuit Design
